Why Your Parliament Should Send  

A (Women Leaders Delegations to Dubai)  

to Participate in the 2024 Congress  


We sincerely appreciate your Parliament efforts in supporting women role models and recognizing their influence on young women leaders, your organization’s dedication to advancing women is evident, as it contributes to the country's global competitiveness, fosters leadership, promotes excellence, and empowers emerging young women leaders to become the next generation of Women Role Models.


In this context, and in the occasion of the International Women’s Day, Datamatix in association with the global government organisation, business corporates, media, global official institutions, and with the support of the Middle East Excellence Awards Institute and with the presence of women role model and leaders, with great pleasure presents the 26th International Women Leaders Congress, on 6-8 March 2024, Dubai, UAE.


On this occasion, with great pleasure we communicate with your Honorable office, requesting your support and approval for forming a women leader’s representative and delegation from the Parliament to participate in the congress and serve as Women Ambassadors for your country and parliament, making a significant impact and contributing to the success of future endeavours, your Parliament delegation presence, will foster the global discussions on empowering young women leaders.


In this context, we encourage the Parliaments and other related organizations, to join the delegation too, to benefit from this congress and other opportunities which the event will provide for women leader’s empowerment specially in the parliaments.


With the participation of distinguished women role models and leaders from the parliament, they serve as your Country Women Ambassadors at this global congress. This endorses your Parliament supportive values and enhances the objective of establishing fruitful relationships with intellectually empowered global women leaders at the congress.


It also fosters the creation of strong and sustainable future partnerships, ultimately deepening bilateral relations between your country and other delegation countries and organizations.


Your Parliament delegation and representative participation can help create and develop the best partnership with the most successful organizations like Dubai and UAE government entities and other countries.


We look forward to welcoming your “Parliament Women Leaders Representative and Delegation”.
